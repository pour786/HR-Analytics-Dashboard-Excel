**                                              **📊 HR Analytics Dashboard (Excel Project)****
 
**📌 Project Overview****
This project focuses on analyzing employee data to identify key factors affecting attrition. The dashboard provides interactive insights into employee count, attrition rate, salary distribution, job roles, and satisfaction levels.

**🎯 Business Problem**
The objective is to understand:
Why employees are leaving the company
Which departments and roles have higher attrition
How salary and job satisfaction impact employee retention

**📂 Dataset**
Total Employees: **10,000**
Includes: Age, Department, Job Role, Salary, Attrition, Satisfaction Level

**⚙️ Tools & Techniques Used**
1_Microsoft Excel
2_Power Query (Data Cleaning)
3_Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
4_Slicers & Dashboard Design
5_VBA (**for buttons like Refresh, Full Screen**)
**
**📊 Key Analysis Performed****

**🔹 Phase 1**  Basic Analysis
Total Employees Count
Attrition Count (Yes/No)
Attrition Rate (%)
**🔹 Phase 2:** Department & Job Role Analysis
Attrition by Department
Attrition by Job Role
**🔹 Phase 3:** Salary Analysis
Average Salary by Department & Role
Attrition by Salary Level
**🔹 Phase 4:** Advanced HR Insights
Attrition by Age Group
Attrition vs Job Satisfaction
Attrition by Gender

**💡 Key Insights**
1_Attrition rate is very high (~48%)
2_Employees with medium salary leave more
3_Young employees (18–30) have higher attrition
4_Low job satisfaction leads to higher attrition

**🛠️ Features of Dashboard**
Interactive slicers (Age, Department, Job Role, Gender)
KPI cards (Total Employees, Attrition Rate, Avg Salary)
VBA buttons (Refresh, Full Screen)
📌 Conclusion
This dashboard helps HR teams identify risk areas and improve employee retention strategies.
